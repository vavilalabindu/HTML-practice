Bangladesh defeated India by five runs in the second ODI to take an unassailable 2-0 lead in the three-match series on Wednesday. Opting to bat, Mehidy Hasan Miraz (100 not out) slammed his maiden ODI century and forged a 148-run stand with Mahmudullah Riyadh (77) to lift Bangladesh to a challenging total of 271/7. The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), which hasn't lost an election in Gujarat since 1995, might cross an all-time record of 149 seats as it leads in 158 out of 182 seats, as per trends as of 11 am. The BJP has been in power in Gujarat for 27 years but has never won an election with such a massive mandate.
